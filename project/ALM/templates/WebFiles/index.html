Sigmoid - Quant Analytics Platform

The idea is to capitalize on special market events and be prepared by taking positions in Equities, Options or futures markets to maximise from the market swings before everyone. The following goal is achieved by backtesting on such events eg. Political events, Storms or natural disasters, Corporate events, Unforeseen events like trade wars, attacks. To make sure the goals are achieved, quantitative research is done on past events fitting similar critertia and the assets which were most volatile in those events. The research is followed by real time extensive backtesting to incorporate trade costs, market volatility and asset behaviour.

Quantitative research methodlogy
  • Factor/Statistical modelling for assets
  • Machine Learning
  • Asset behaviors, correlations & arbs
  • Hedging and risk analytics
  • Arbitrage Opportunities due to market swings
  • Amplifying hidden Patterns & Singals
  • Sentiment Analysis
  • Python/R/Excel/BI tools combination for visualization
Qualitative research methodlogy
  • Study factors causing market moving events
  • Financial impact on Balance Sheets
  • Current market conditions
  • Probability of same market moving events happening again
  • Exploring Interest rates, commodities, bonds, equities, options behavior
